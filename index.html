About Me

I'm an undergraduate student in Information Technology at the Digital Metropolis Institute (IMD/UFRN). I conduct undergraduate research in Bioinformatics as a member of the Systems Biology Group , esearch at the interface of computer science, statistics, and molecular biology, with an emphasis on applying computational methods to biological data analysis.

In bioinformatics, I work with transcriptomic analyses and I am currently expanding my skills in R package development and the construction of computational pipelines. In software development, I have experience with data analysis using R and SQL, as well as knowledge in backend development with PHP and Laravel.
